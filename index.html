Slapshot Features

  • Be more productive

    Need to take several screenshots? Let Slapshot keep trak of them until you need them!

  • Great configuration options

    Tell Slapshot where you want your screenshots saved.

  • To the cloud!

    Since Slapshot saves your screenshots to disk, you can easily add them as attachments without needing graphics editing software.

  • Minimalistic Layout

    Slapshot runs quietly in your System Tray so you don't have to worry about giving up valuable screen real estate. Just set it and forget it!
